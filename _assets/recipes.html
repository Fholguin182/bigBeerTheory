Nacho Mommas Jalape√±o Poppers

Ingredients

  • 12 jalepino's
  • 12 bacon slices
  • 24oz of cream cheese
  • 1 16oz can of nacho cheese
  • wood chips
  • 12 tooth picks
  • pale ale
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 ziploc bags
  • Vertical grill safe stand

Preperation

Begin by soaking the wood chips in of Pale Ale for 30 minutes.Hydradied wood chips give off more smoke. Soak toothpicks in water. This prevents burning during cooking. Cut the tops off the Jalepino's. Take out or leave the seeds (more seeds; more heat). Dust the bacon stips with cracked black pepper and a pinch of salt. Wrap the bacon around Jalepino's, tie with toothpicks. Place jalepino's on grill safe vertical stand. Put cheese in there repective ziploc bags, cut off bottom corner for piping. Pipe Jalepinos with cheese, evenly.

Cooking Directions

Pre-heat BQQ. add beer soaked wood chips to hot coals. Place Jalepino Poppers on grill and cover for 45-60 minuites pending on heat of the fire
